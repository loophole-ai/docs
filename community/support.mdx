Loophole's a community project, so help comes from a few different places. Here's where to look depending on what you need.

## Quick questions

Join our [Discord](https://discord.gg/loophole). It's the fastest way to get a human to answer you. Lots of friendly folks hang out there — ask anything.

## "Is this a bug?"

Check the [Troubleshooting page](/guides/troubleshooting) first. If that doesn't fix it, [open an issue](/community/reporting-issues).

## "How do I do X?"

Search the docs (there's a search bar up top). Most common questions are covered somewhere in here.

If it's not, ask on Discord or [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/loophole-ai/loophole-ide/discussions).

## "I have an idea"

Check [Feature requests](/community/feature-requests).

## "I want to help out"

Check [Contributing](/community/contributing). We'd love to have you.

## Where not to ask

Please don't DM the maintainers on social media or email. We're a small team and answering individually doesn't scale. Public channels mean other people with the same question can find the answer later.

## Response times

We try to respond to everything within a few days. Sometimes it takes longer — we have day jobs too. Be patient and we'll get to you.
