Loophole is an AI code editor. Think of it like your normal editor, but with an AI that actually understands your codebase and can write code with you, fix bugs, and answer questions about your project.

It's open source, free to use, and built for developers who want a fast, friendly coding experience.

## What you can do with Loophole

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Chat with your code" icon="comments" href="/features/ai-chat">
    Ask questions about any file, function, or your whole project.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Smart autocomplete" icon="wand-magic-sparkles" href="/features/autocomplete">
    Get suggestions that actually fit your codebase, not generic ones.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Agent mode" icon="robot" href="/features/agent-mode">
    Let Loophole make changes across multiple files for you.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Inline edits" icon="pen-to-square" href="/features/inline-edits">
    Highlight code, describe what you want, and let Loophole rewrite it.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
